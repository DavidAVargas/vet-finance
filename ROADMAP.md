# Vet Finance — Project Roadmap

> Free financial education for military members and veterans. Our mission is to reduce veteran homelessness and set service members up for financial success — during and after service.

## Phase 2 — Military Credit Card Line

### Research & Partnerships
- [ ] Research banking partners (Bank of America, Chase)
- [ ] Define card benefits and eligibility per tier
- [ ] Legal & compliance review

### Card Tiers
- [ ] **Combat Black** — elite card, combat veterans only
- [ ] **Platinum** — all military members
- [ ] **Supporter** — civilians supporting troops (~$100/yr annual fee)

### Supporter Card Revenue Model
- [ ] Revenue from Supporter card funds veteran perks (travel, gear discounts, getaways)
- [ ] "Thank you for your service" on every purchase receipt
